REELS: THE $50 BILLION VIDEO FORMAT
================================================================================

A single video format inside Meta's apps now generates more annual revenue
than Nike, Coca-Cola, or Netflix.

================================================================================
PART 1: REVENUE GROWTH TIMELINE
================================================================================

Aug 2020    Launch              Reels launches on Instagram as TikTok response
Feb 2021    Expansion           Reels comes to Facebook
Jun 2021    Monetization        Reels Ads launch globally
Oct 2022    $3B run rate        First disclosed figure; 140B daily plays
Jul 2023    $10B run rate       75%+ advertisers using Reels; 3.3x growth in 9 months
Q4 2023     37% of IG time      37% of US Instagram time spent on Reels
Q3 2024     19% ad impressions  Reels = 19% of Instagram ad impressions
Q4 2024     35% ad inventory    Reels = 35% of Instagram ad inventory
Q1 2025     Video surge         Video time on Instagram up 30%+ YoY
Q2 2025     50% of ads          Majority of IG ads now run on Reels; 46% of US IG time
Q3 2025     $50B run rate       Zuckerberg announces $50B; Reels = majority of IG time

GROWTH TRAJECTORY
-----------------
$0 to $3B:      2 years (Aug 2020 - Oct 2022)
$3B to $10B:    9 months (Oct 2022 - Jul 2023) = 3.3x
$10B to $50B:   27 months (Jul 2023 - Oct 2025) = 5x

Overall: $0 to $50B in 5 years
Comparison: TikTok took 8+ years to reach ~$17B

Q3 2025 KEY METRICS (from earnings call)
----------------------------------------
- Total Meta revenue: $51.2B (up 26% YoY)
- Reels run rate: $50B annually
- Ad impressions: +14% YoY
- Average price per ad: +10% YoY
- Reels = majority of time spent on Instagram
- Video consumption on IG: +30% YoY
- AI ad tools run rate: $60B+

REELS vs TIKTOK FOR ADVERTISERS
-------------------------------
Advantage: Reels

- Superior retargeting through Meta's ecosystem
- Stronger for bottom-of-funnel conversions

Advantage: TikTok
- Stronger organic virality potential
- Better for brand awareness / top-of-funnel
- Younger demographic skew
- More authentic/raw content expectations
